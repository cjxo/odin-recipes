Miso Soup

Description

This miso soup recipe is a healthy and comforting way to get warm during the colder rainy days.

What To Know

  • Total Time To Cook: 20 min
  • Servings: 4
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 pc Knorr Chicken Cube
  • 3 tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 cup silken tofu, cubed
  • 2 pcs green portion of green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring water and Knorr Chicken Cube to boil. Reduce heat to a simmer.
  2. Scoop out 1 cup of the chicken stock into a bowl and stir in the miso paste.
  3. Pour the miso and stock mixture back into the pot.
  4. Add cubed tofu and sliced green onions into the pot.
  5. Gently simmer for 2 minutes and serve.
  6. Enjoy your warm bowls of miso soup made with the simplest ingredients!
